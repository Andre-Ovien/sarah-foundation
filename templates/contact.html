Frequently Asked Questions

At Sarah Foundation, we believe clarity builds trust. Our Frequently Asked Questions section is here to help you understand who we are, what we do, and how you can be part of our mission to empower individuals towards independence.

What is the Sarah Foundation?

Sarah Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and empowering vulnerable individuals, helping them achieve independence and dignity.

Who do you help?

We focus on individuals and communities facing hardship, especially the less privileged, providing resources, skills, and opportunities to improve their lives.

What kind of support do you offer?

Our programs include skill development, community outreach, educational support, and access to basic needs — all designed to foster self-reliance.

How can I get involved?

You can volunteer your time, donate funds or materials, or partner with us to expand the reach of our impact.

How are donations used?

Every donation is directed towards programs and initiatives that directly benefit our target communities, with transparent reporting on impact.

How can I contact Sarah Foundation?

You can reach us via our website, email, or social media platforms — we’re always happy to connect with supporters and partners.

ABOUT THE FOUNDATION

Who We Are

Unleashing Potential with Creative Strategy

Sarah Foundation, for Children and Adult Wellness and Mental Health Awareness is a non-governmental, non-profit organization committed to supporting and empowering vulnerable individuals within various communities in Lagos, Nigeria, Creating a significant and lasting positive impact on the lives of those who need it most. Each year, we organize several impactful outreach programs, essential support, educational resources and skill development opportunities to children, adults, and widows in underserved areas across different communities in Lagos, Nigeria throughout the year. Sarah Foundation is designed to make a tangible difference in the lives of children, widows, and other disadvantaged groups.

Our Mission

Supporting and empowering vulnerable individuals in society.

Our Vision

To cultivate good independence in people in society.

Join Hands with Us to Make a Lasting Impact

  • Provide essential items for orphans and children in need
  • Empower young people with sustainable skills
  • Uplift widows and restore their dignity
  • Promote education and reduce school drop-out rates in vulnerable communities
